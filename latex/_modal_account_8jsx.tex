\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
@component Modal\+Account 

A modal for displaying or managing account-\/related features (e.\+g., profile, settings).

This modal appears over the screen and can be closed by clicking the background. Currently, it renders an empty container but can be expanded to include account options.
